Frequently Asked Questions

Is Student Learning Hub free to use?

Yes. All video lessons, study notes, and practice papers are completely free for students in Sri Lanka.

Which grades are covered?

We cover Grades 1 to 13, including GCE O/L and A/L preparation, for both the Sri Lanka National Curriculum and the British Curriculum (Edexcel and Cambridge).

What subjects are available?

Mathematics, Science, English Language & Literature, ICT, Sinhala, Tamil, History, Geography, Civic Education, Health & PE, Religion, GIT, BIT, FIT, and software development.

Are past papers available?

Yes. Term 2 and Term 3 past papers from 2018 to 2024 are available for multiple subjects under Sri Lanka's National Curriculum.
